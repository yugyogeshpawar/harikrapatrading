Shipping Policy

At Hari Krapa Trading, we are committed to providing efficient and reliable shipping services for all our wholesale grain and agro products. This policy outlines our shipping procedures, delivery times, and other important information.

Order Processing Time

All orders are processed within 2-3 business days (excluding weekends and holidays) after receiving your order confirmation email. You will receive another notification when your order has shipped.

Please note that during peak seasons or unforeseen circumstances, processing times may be slightly longer. We appreciate your patience.

Shipping Rates and Delivery Estimates

Shipping charges for your order will be calculated and displayed at checkout. We offer various shipping options to meet your needs:

  • Standard Shipping: Estimated delivery within 5-7 business days.
  • Expedited Shipping: Estimated delivery within 2-4 business days.
  • Freight Shipping: For large bulk orders, freight shipping options will be discussed and arranged directly with you to ensure the most cost-effective and timely delivery.

Delivery estimates are from the time of shipment, not from the time of order placement.

Shipping Destinations

We ship to all major locations across India. For international shipping inquiries, please contact us directly at sundramsahu999@gmail.com to discuss feasibility and arrangements.

Order Tracking

Once your order has shipped, you will receive an email notification from us which will include a tracking number you can use to check its status. Please allow 48 hours for the tracking information to become available.

If you haven’t received your order within [X] days of receiving your shipping confirmation email, please contact us at sundramsahu999@gmail.com with your name and order number, and we will look into it for you.

Customs, Duties, and Taxes

Hari Krapa Trading is not responsible for any customs and taxes applied to your order. All fees imposed during or after shipping are the responsibility of the customer (tariffs, taxes, etc.).

Damages

Hari Krapa Trading is not liable for any products damaged or lost during shipping. If you received your order damaged, please contact the shipment carrier to file a claim. Please save all packaging materials and damaged goods before filing a claim.

Contact Us

For any questions about our shipping policy, please contact us at Contact Us page or email us at sundramsahu999@gmail.com.

Hari Krapa Trading is your trusted partner for wholesale grain and agro products. We provide high-quality grains to meet your business needs.
